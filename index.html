Your Gateway to Southeast Asian Markets

DIVOOST is a Vietnam-based global e-commerce company specializing in connecting Korean brands with Southeast Asian consumers. We provide end-to-end solutions from certification and market testing to large-scale live commerce operations.

Based in Hanoi, we operate as an independent partner focused on delivering authentic Korean products and experiences to the rapidly growing Vietnamese and Southeast Asian markets.
