FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does ICP filing take?

Typically 15~30 days. We need cooperation with company registration certificate, official seal, and domain ownership verification. Our Shandong Yiji China HQ handles the filing directly.

Is global CDN sufficient without China hosting?

CDN improves things to a degree, but some pages (media, payments) are more stable with in-China servers. We recommend the right setup based on traffic and business model.

Can we just translate an existing site to Chinese?

Translation alone isn't enough. China users expect very different information density, payment UX, SNS integration, and ad-law-compliant copy. Localized copy and design directly impact conversion.

What about maintenance cost?

Monthly maintenance + content update packages (around $500~$1,500/mo), or per-hour ops available. Package discounts apply when combined with marketing.
