TikTok Shop Vietnam GMV Max — Case Analysis

GMV Max is TikTok Shop's automated bidding system. We analyzed 90 days of data from 3 Korean brands operating in Vietnam to find what actually works.

3.8xAvg ROAS
42%Cost reduction vs manual
17 daysAvg learning period

Brand 1: K-Beauty Skincare

Premium serum line, AOV $35. GMV Max started slow but reached 4.2x ROAS in week 3.

Key takeaway:

  • Initial 14-day learning period needs minimum $1,500 daily budget
  • Don't pause campaigns during learning — kills progress
  • Best creatives: before/after with Vietnamese voiceover

Brand 2: Korean Snacks

Low AOV ($8), high volume. GMV Max struggled at first — algorithm needs higher AOV for stable learning.

Solution: bundled $8 items into $25 multi-pack SKUs. GMV Max immediately optimized — 2.8x ROAS within 10 days.

Brand 3: Fashion Accessories

Mid-AOV ($22), seasonal trends. Best ROAS of all three (4.5x) by leveraging live commerce + GMV Max combo.

GMV Max Best Practices

  1. Minimum AOV $20 for stable optimization
  2. 14-day learning period — don't touch settings
  3. Run 5-10 ad creatives in parallel for variation
  4. Vietnamese voiceover beats Korean/English by 3.2x CTR
  5. Combine with live commerce for maximum lift

When NOT to Use GMV Max

If your daily budget is below $500, stick with manual bidding. The algorithm needs volume to learn.
