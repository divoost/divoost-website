The 1:10:50 Rule for KOL × KOC Mix

After running 47 campaigns across beauty, fashion, food, and home categories on Xiaohongshu, we found one mix consistently outperforms all others: 1 KOL + 10 mid-tier + 50 KOCs.

Why This Ratio Works

Each tier serves a different psychological purpose in the buyer's journey:

1 Mega-KOL (500k+ followers)

  • Awareness — brand discovery
  • Initial credibility signal
  • Sets price perception

10 Mid-tier KOLs (50k-500k)

  • Consideration — detailed reviews
  • Use case demonstration
  • Long-form content (3-5 min videos)

50 KOCs (1k-50k followers)

  • Social proof — authenticity
  • UGC volume for SEO
  • Comment / save signals for algorithm

Budget Allocation

For a $30,000 monthly Xiaohongshu budget:

  • 1 Mega-KOL: $12,000 (40%)
  • 10 Mid-tier: $13,500 ($1,350 each, 45%)
  • 50 KOCs: $4,500 ($90 each, 15%)

Scheduling — Wave Strategy

Don't release all 61 posts at once. Wave them: KOCs first (week 1), Mid-tier (week 2), Mega-KOL (week 3). This creates organic momentum.

Real Campaign Example

A Korean skincare brand spent $30k on this mix. Results after 30 days:

  • Reach: 8.4M Xiaohongshu users
  • Search ranking: #1 for primary keyword
  • TMall sales: +320% MoM
  • ROI: 4.7x (vs 1.2x with single KOL)
