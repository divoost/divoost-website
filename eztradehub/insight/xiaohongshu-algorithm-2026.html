2026 Xiaohongshu Algorithm — Keyword SEO Is Half the Battle

Xiaohongshu has undergone significant algorithm changes in the past 6 months. Brands that have not adapted have seen 40-60% drops in organic reach.

↓ 47%Average organic reach drop
↑ 32%Search-driven traffic growth
3xKeyword-optimized post CTR

1. Search Has Overtaken Discovery Feed

In Q1 2026, search-driven traffic on Xiaohongshu surpassed discovery feed traffic for the first time. This means keyword optimization is now more important than visual hooks.

Posts with optimized keywords in the title now get 3x the CTR of visually-driven posts.

2. Title + First Line = 80% of Ranking

The new algorithm weighs the title and first 100 characters of the body as 80% of relevance scoring. Hashtags now contribute less than 10%.

What to do:

  • Front-load 2-3 primary keywords in your title
  • Repeat keywords naturally in the first sentence
  • Use long-tail keywords (3-5 words) over single broad terms

3. KOC Posts > KOL Posts

The algorithm now favors authentic, lower-follower KOC content. KOL posts (500k+ followers) get penalized if engagement rate drops below 3%.

4. Video Posts Get 2.3x More Reach

Xiaohongshu is pushing video format aggressively. 15-30 second vertical videos get 2.3x more reach than static image posts on average.

5. Comments Matter More Than Likes

In the new engagement scoring, comments are weighted 4x more than likes, and 8x more than saves. Reply to every comment in the first hour for maximum boost.

Action Checklist

  1. Audit your top 10 posts — are titles keyword-optimized?
  2. Plan video content for next month (40% of posts minimum)
  3. Set up comment-reply within 1 hour SLA
  4. Shift KOL budget 30% → KOC budget
